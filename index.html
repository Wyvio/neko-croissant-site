Travel to Paris

As the capital of France, Paris is a vibrant hub of activity, a historical yet innovative center of art.

Eat good food!

The crisp crunch of the baguette as it is snapped in half is the telltale sign of a fresh boulangerie's artisitic works.

Have a taste of other French classics:

  • Un croque-monsieur
  • Des escargots
  • Les macarons

See great places!

The Eiffel Tower is the icon of Paris and is breathtaking at night.

Don't forget to also visit:

  • The Louvre
  • La Seine
  • The Notre Dame

Hear fun songs!

Iconic in memes and internet culture, accordion music is frequently associated in French stereotypes.
